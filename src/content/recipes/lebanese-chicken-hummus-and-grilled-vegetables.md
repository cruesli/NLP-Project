ingredients: |-
  - 400g Chicken thighs
  - 200g Hummus
  - 1 Bell pepper cut into chunks
  - 1 Onion cut into chunks
  - 2-3 garlic cloves thinly sliced
  - 1 Lemon cut in half
  - Spices for marinating chicken
  - Olive oil
---
1. Marinate chicken in spices, salt and olive oil for ideally at least 20 min
2. Heat a frying pan over high heat with olive oil
3. Sear the chicken until golden brown on both sides and cooked through
4. Take out the chicken and add in the peppers, onion and lemon (open side down). 
5. Season with salt and pepper
6. Cook until the vegetables soften and get some colour. Take the lemon out once it is caremalized on the open side.
7. When the vegetables are very close to done, add the garlic and cook for 1 minute
8. Slice the chicken into strips before serving

To serve add a good amount of hummus in the bottom of a plate or bowl and put the vegetables and then chicken on top. 
Finish with a big squeeze of caramelized lemon.
