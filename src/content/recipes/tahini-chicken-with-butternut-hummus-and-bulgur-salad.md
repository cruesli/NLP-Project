1. Marinate chicken

2. Preheat oven to 200 C

3. Cut the butternut squash in half and place it on a tray, filling on of the holes with the garlic cloves and covering them with olive oil. Then bake in the oven.

4. Cook chickpeas and bulgur

5. When the butternut squash is almost done, put the chicken on a tray and roast in oven

6. Take out the butternut squash once done and add it and the garlic to a food processor along with the chickpeas, some Tahini, a glugg of olive oil, a squeeze of lemon and a good pinch of salt. Blend until smooth.

7. Chop up parsley and finely dice the red onion

8. Combine the bulgur with the onion and parsley, along with a glugg of olive oil, a squeeze of lemon and a pinch of salt. Toss to combine.

9. Once the chicken is nicely charred and cooked through, take it out and let it rest a bit before serving.

Serve with an extra wedge of lemon to squeeze on top.
