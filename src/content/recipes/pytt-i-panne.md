1. Cut the vegetables and sausages into 1cm cubes

2. Heat a pan over medium high heat with some oil

3. Putt everything into the pan and fry until you get some nice color

4. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and optionally thyme, rosemary or oregano.

5. Serve with 0.5kg ketchup
