---
title: Pasta Bolognese
cuisine: italian
foodType: dinner
tags:
  - family
  - freezer-friendly
servings: 4
totalTimeMinutes: 60
image: /images/recipes/pasta-bolognese.jpg
ingredients: |-
  * 500g minced beef

  * 1 onion, diced
  * 2 cloves garlic, minced
  * 1 can chopped tomatoes
  * Salt and pepper
---

1. Sauté onion and garlic.
2. Brown the beef.
3. Add tomatoes and simmer 30–40 minutes.
4. Boil pasta and serve.
